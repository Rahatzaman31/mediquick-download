Help Center

Find answers and get support

Getting Started

How to Download and Install

Download the MediQuick APK from our website and install it on your Android device. Make sure to enable "Install from Unknown Sources" in your device settings.

Creating an Account

Tap "Sign Up" on the welcome screen, enter your phone number, verify it with the OTP code, and complete your profile with basic information.

Using the App

Finding a Doctor

Use the search feature to find doctors by name, specialty, or location. You can filter results and view detailed profiles before booking.

Booking Appointments

Select a doctor, choose an available time slot, confirm your appointment details, and receive instant confirmation.

Canceling Appointments

Go to your appointments section, select the appointment you want to cancel, and tap "Cancel Appointment". Please cancel at least 2 hours before the scheduled time.

Emergency Services

Access emergency services including ambulance requests, blood bank, and hospital information from the home screen.

Account & Settings

Resetting Your Password

Tap "Forgot Password" on the login screen, enter your registered phone number, and follow the verification process to create a new password.

Updating Profile Information

Go to the Profile section, tap "Edit Profile", update your information, and save changes.

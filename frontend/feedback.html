Feedback Guidelines

To help us address your feedback effectively:

  • Be specific about what you're referring to
  • Provide examples when possible
  • Include your contact information if you'd like a response
  • Be constructive and respectful
  • Mention the app version you're using

What We Do With Your Feedback

  1. Review: We carefully read every piece of feedback
  2. Analyze: We look for patterns and common themes
  3. Prioritize: We determine which suggestions align with our roadmap
  4. Implement: We work on incorporating valuable suggestions
  5. Update: We release improvements in app updates

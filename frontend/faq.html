Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

General Questions

What is MediQuick?

MediQuick is a comprehensive healthcare platform that connects patients with doctors, provides emergency services, facilitates medicine orders, and offers various healthcare solutions in Rangpur, Bangladesh.

Is MediQuick free to use?

Yes, downloading and using the MediQuick app is completely free. You only pay for the actual medical services you receive from healthcare providers.

Which devices are supported?

MediQuick is available for Android devices running Android 5.0 or higher.

Appointments

How do I book an appointment?

Search for a doctor, select an available time slot, confirm your details, and your appointment will be booked instantly.

Can I cancel or reschedule an appointment?

Yes, you can cancel or reschedule appointments through the app. Please do so at least 2 hours before the scheduled time.

Will I receive appointment reminders?

Yes, we send notifications to remind you about upcoming appointments.

What if a doctor cancels my appointment?

You will be notified immediately and can book another appointment with the same or a different doctor.

Payments

How do I make payments?

Payments can be made through the app using mobile banking, cards, or at the doctor's office as per your preference.

Are consultations refundable?

Refund policies vary by healthcare provider. Please check individual provider policies or contact support.

Emergency Services

Can I request an ambulance through the app?

Yes, the app provides quick access to ambulance services. You can request an ambulance from the Emergency Services section.

How do I find blood donors?

Use the Blood Bank feature to search for donors by blood type and location.

Privacy & Security

Is my medical information secure?

Yes, we use industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your personal and medical information.

Who can see my medical records?

Only you and the healthcare providers you book appointments with can access your medical information.
